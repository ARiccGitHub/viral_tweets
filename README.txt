Overview:

In this project, Twitter Classification Cumulative Project, 
I use real tweets to find patterns in the way people use social media. 

There are two parts to this project:
• Part-1: Viral Tweets (This Section).
  Predict Viral Tweets, using a K-Nearest Neighbor classifier to predict whether or not a tweet will go viral.
Note: I went be beyond the Codecademy project's goal, by evaluating three different models.
• Part-2: Classifying Tweets, 
  using Naïve Bayes classifiers to predict whether a tweet was sent from New York City, London, or Paris.
